Education Background

My academic journey has been a path of growth, discovery, and hard work, leading me to where I am today. I began my foundational years at Heshima Academy, where I completed my nursery and pre-unit education, laying the groundwork for my academic aspirations. In 2010, I joined Kisii Primary School, where I studied from Standard 1 through Standard 6, honing my skills and developing a passion for learning. In my final two years of primary education, I transitioned to Nyaramba D.O.K, where I completed Classes 7 and 8. My dedication and perseverance bore fruit when I performed exceptionally well in my KCPE exams, earning me a place at Kisii High School. From 2018 to March 2022, I attended Kisii High School, where I embraced academic challenges and worked diligently, achieving a commendable grade. This success paved the way for my admission to the prestigious Technical University of Mombasa (TUM). Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Information Communication Technology (ICT) at TUM. Here, I continue to build on my knowledge and skills, driven by my passion for technology and innovation. My educational journey has been a testament to my commitment to growth and excellence, and I am excited about the future as I continue to explore and contribute to the dynamic world of Information Technology.
