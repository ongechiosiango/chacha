Education Background

I began my educational journey at Kisauni, where I completed my nursery and pre-unit classes. In 2010, I transitioned to Kisii Primary School, where I studied from Standard 1 through Standard 6. I then moved to Migori D.E.B for my final two years of primary education, completing classes 7 and 8. My hard work paid off when I performed well in my KCPE exams, which opened the door for me to attend Nyambaria High School from 2018 to March 2022. There, I achieved a commendable grade that allowed me to further my studies at the Technical University of Mombasa (TUM), where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Information Communication Technology (I.C.T).
